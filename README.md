## Hello!

I'm a geomorphologist and PhD student at CU Boulder (Geological Sciences · INSTAAR). I study how glaciers and frozen ground move, and what that motion records about a changing climate.

**Currently working on**
- Wave ogives on the Juneau Icefield
- Rock glacier dynamics and thermal resilience in the San Juan Mountains
- Small, tractable ice-sheet models of the Mid-Pleistocene Transition

I'm happiest building simple models and doing fieldwork (GNSS, seismic, UAV).
